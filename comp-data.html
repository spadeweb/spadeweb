The WeAreUs data set is comprised of notifications paired with user contexts. The data was gathered during an in-the-wild study of user behaviour toward incoming mobile notifications. On each delivery of a notification, features such as app and subject were recorded as was the user's action toward the notification: open or dismiss. Other contextual features relating to the user, such as noise levels and type of location, were also recorded both at the time of delivery and at the time of action. More information regarding the features collected during the WeAreUs study can be found on the project website.
